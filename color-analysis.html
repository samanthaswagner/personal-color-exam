Want to measure your real range?

This part is completely optional. You already have a full result from your answers — photos just make the range more precise. Upload 1–4 photos: one measures your coloring; two or more across different looks (no-makeup vs. full glam, light vs. dark hair) measures how far your look actually swings. For each photo you'll tap three spots. Best results: natural daylight, looking at the camera.

🔒 Your photos stay in your browser — they're never uploaded, never saved to disk, and are not included when you send your results. Closing this tab erases them. Only the text summary is ever shared.
